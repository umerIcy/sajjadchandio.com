Symptoms

We offer consultation and endoscopy for investigation and management of a variety of symptoms. Click on an organ to see a list of symptoms we can assist you with.

NOTE: The list provided is not exhaustive. If you have a symptom that does not fit well into a category, get in touch with our team.

Liver/Hepatology

Small and Large Bowel

Pancreas

Biliary system

Liver / Hepatology

  • Jaundice (yellowing of skin or eyes)
  • Abnormal liver function tests
  • Fatty liver disease
  • Hepatitis (viral or autoimmune)
  • Liver cirrhosis
  • Unexplained weight loss with liver abnormalities
  • Right upper abdominal pain
  • Ascites (fluid accumulation in abdomen)

Small and Large Bowel

  • Chronic diarrhoea or constipation
  • Change in bowel habits
  • Rectal bleeding or blood in stool
  • Abdominal pain or cramping
  • Bloating and excessive gas
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Iron deficiency anaemia
  • Suspected inflammatory bowel disease (IBD)
  • Coeliac disease screening
  • Bowel cancer screening

Pancreas

  • Epigastric (upper abdominal) pain radiating to the back
  • Recurrent pancreatitis
  • Chronic pancreatitis symptoms
  • Pancreatic cysts
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • New-onset diabetes with pancreatic symptoms
  • Steatorrhoea (fatty stools)

Biliary System

  • Gallstone-related symptoms
  • Bile duct stones
  • Biliary colic (pain after eating fatty foods)
  • Obstructive jaundice
  • Cholangitis (bile duct infection)
  • Abnormal imaging of bile ducts
  • Post-cholecystectomy symptoms

Stomach

  • Heartburn / acid reflux (GERD)
  • Difficulty swallowing (dysphagia)
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Upper abdominal pain or discomfort
  • Gastric ulcers
  • Helicobacter pylori infection
  • Early satiety (feeling full quickly)
  • Unexplained anaemia
  • Barrett's oesophagus surveillance

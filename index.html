Welcome to Chandio Gastro & Endoscopy Center

We are a Gastroenterology and Endoscopy practice dedicated to providing advanced digestive health care.

Dr. Sajjad Hussain Chandio (MBBS, FCPS – Gastroenterology) is our lead Gastroenterologist and Endoscopy Specialist. He is a highly qualified specialist with extensive experience in the diagnosis and management of gastrointestinal diseases. Dr. Sajjad Hussain Chandio is committed to providing high-quality, patient-centered care using modern endoscopic techniques.

He focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of digestive system disorders including reflux disease, liver conditions, gastrointestinal infections, and other digestive health issues. His goal is to provide effective and minimally invasive solutions to improve patient health and quality of life.

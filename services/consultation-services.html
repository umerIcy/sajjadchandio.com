About our consultation services

We offer comprehensive gastroenterology consultations for patients referred by their general practitioners or other specialists. Our consultations provide expert assessment, diagnosis, and management plans for a wide range of digestive health conditions.

Consultations are available at multiple locations for your convenience.

What to expect during your consultation

Your consultation will typically last 20 to 30 minutes. The doctor will take a detailed history of your symptoms, review any previous investigations, and perform a physical examination if necessary.

Based on the assessment, the doctor may recommend further investigations such as blood tests, imaging, or endoscopic procedures. A comprehensive management plan will be discussed with you.

Conditions we manage

  • Gastro-oesophageal reflux disease (GORD)
  • Peptic ulcer disease
  • Coeliac disease
  • Inflammatory bowel disease (Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis)
  • Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS)
  • Liver disease and abnormal liver function tests
  • Iron deficiency anaemia
  • Gastrointestinal cancers
  • Swallowing difficulties
  • Chronic abdominal pain
  • Chronic diarrhoea and constipation
  • Helicobacter pylori infection

Preparing for your appointment

  • Bring a valid referral from your GP or specialist
  • Bring a list of all current medications
  • Bring any recent investigation results (blood tests, imaging, pathology reports)

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time to complete registration forms.

After your consultation

A detailed report will be sent to your referring doctor outlining the findings and management plan.

If further investigations are required, our team will help arrange these. Follow-up appointments can be scheduled as needed.
